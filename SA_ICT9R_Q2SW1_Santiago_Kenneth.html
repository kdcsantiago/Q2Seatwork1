Types of Dogs
Dog Breed Description Image
Golden Retriever These types of dogs are usually medium sized, energetic. This breed of dogs are usually known for its gold coat of fur.
Poodle Poodles Come in all sorts of sizes. There are the Standard Poodles, the Medium Poodles, Miniature Poodles, and the Toy Poodles. These dogs are usually easier to tame than others.
Siberian Husky This types of dogs are usually taller and bigger than other breeds. These dogs usually like being in the cold since they have thick fur.
Shih Tzu Shih Tzus are usually small and cute. They are more of a toy dog than a guard dog.
Pomeranian These dogs are usually very small. They also have different sizes like the Standard Pomeranian, and the Teacup Pomeranian.
